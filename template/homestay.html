Yog Homestay

Yog Homestay is located at a distance of 10 km from Ranikhet in Majkhali, Uttarakhand, India. It is a roadside homestay which will offer you great services and breathtaking Himalayan views. The view of Trishul mountain, Nandghunti and Nanda Devi Parvat ranges are a sight to behold. The guesthouse consists of two bedrooms, a living room, kitchen and dining area with all the basic amenities including WiFi too. It is perfect for a family of five to six people. The local market is close by.
